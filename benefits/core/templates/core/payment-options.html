Cal-ITP is a new program serving all Californians. In order to activate your discount, you need a bank card that has the contactless symbol on it. The contactless symbol is four curved lines and it looks like this:

A contactless bank card can be:

  • A credit card
  • A debit card
  • A prepaid card (if it includes a Visa logo)

We are working to add additional options for people who do not have access to a bank card. Please check back on this website, or get in touch with your local transit provider.
